Instructions

Pick a unique username with a maximum of 20 characters and click Sign Up.

If your username is unique, you will be signed up and be given two keys. A PGP private key, you will need that to redeem the prize copy it and save to a file. And a Public Key Hash that is used to prove you own that username and serves as a password to sign in, both keys will never be shown again, save them.

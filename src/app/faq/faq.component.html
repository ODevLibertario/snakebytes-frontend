FAQ
What is this website?
SnakeBytes is a browser based game that pays a small number of satoshis everyday to the top player in the leaderboard.
How do I play?
First sign in, you'll need to enter an unique username and the game will generate a PGP private key and the public key hash for you, store both and use the username and public key hash to sign in. After you are signed in head to the Play! tab and start playing, the game is classic snake controlled by the arrow keys (mobile is not supported).
PGP?
Yes! You will need knowledge of PGP and Cashu to receive the prize if you become the leader of the leaderboard, google it up, the prize is a PGP encrypted Cashu token encrypted with your key if the prize is yours. This is the mint to redeem your token once you decrypt it: https://8333.space:3338
Do the Leaderboards reset?
Yes! After every prize is paid to give the chance for new players.
